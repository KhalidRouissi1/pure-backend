# Pure Privacy Policy

Effective July 13, 2026.

Pure collects account and profile information, saved addresses, orders, favorites, reviews, seller applications, and content users submit. Location and photo access occur only when users choose features requiring them. Data is used to authenticate users, operate and secure the marketplace, fulfil orders, provide support, and comply with law.

Order and contact information is shared with independent sellers and service providers when required to fulfil a request. Infrastructure providers process data for Pure. Pure does not sell personal information.

Information is retained while an account is active and when reasonably required for accounting, disputes, fraud prevention, security, and legal obligations. Users can update their profile or permanently delete their account inside Settings. Optional device permissions can be withdrawn in system settings.

Privacy requests: support@pure.app.
